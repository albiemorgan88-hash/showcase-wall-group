# Wall Group Research - March 2026

## Business Overview
**The Wall Group** is a retail empire owned by Gavan and Helen Wall, operating across North and West Belfast. Founded by former criminal defence barrister Gavan Wall, who transitioned from law to entrepreneurship.

### Current Portfolio (as of Nov 2024):
- **6 stores total** (originally 4, expanded to 6 with recent £4M investment)
- **140+ employees**
- **2+ million customers annually** 
- Mix of SPAR, EuroSPAR, Subway, YoggieBerrie (their own frozen yogurt brand), Post Office services

### Store Locations:
1. **Landscape Filling Station** - 95 Crumlin Road, Belfast BT14 6AD (opened 2022, £3M investment, 30 jobs)
2. **Flax EuroSPAR** - Ardoyne (opened Nov 2024, £4M investment, community supermarket)
3. **Multiple SPAR stores** across North & West Belfast
4. **Subway franchises** integrated within stores
5. **YoggieBerrie** outlets - their proprietary frozen yogurt/ice cream concept

### Key Business Facts:
- **Food Hygiene Rating**: 5/5 (Very Good) - excellent operational standards
- **Investment Scale**: £7M+ invested in recent expansions (2022-2024)
- **Growth Trajectory**: Rapid expansion from startup to 6-store chain in ~10 years
- **Community Focus**: "At the Heart of the Community" - strong local presence
- **Multi-format**: Convenience + fuel + food service + proprietary brands

### Target Market:
- North & West Belfast communities
- Convenience shoppers needing fuel + groceries
- Quick service food customers (Subway/coffee/frozen treats)
- Post Office service users
- Local families and commuters

## Digital Presence Analysis

### Current Website (wallgroup.co.uk):
- **Built on**: BaseKit/Website Builder platform
- **Content**: Minimal - just community messaging and basic branding
- **Missing**: Store locator, online ordering, detailed services info
- **SEO Score**: Poor (estimated 2/10)

### Competitive Landscape:
- **7-Eleven Australia**: Strong digital presence with 7NOW delivery app
- **Centra Ireland**: Modern website with store locators and promotions
- **SPAR**: Corporate focus on "Customer First" store innovation

## Growth Opportunities Identified:

1. **Digital Transformation**: Massive gap vs modern convenience chains
2. **Online Ordering**: Zero click & collect or delivery capability
3. **Multi-location Management**: No unified digital presence across 6 stores
4. **Customer Loyalty**: No visible loyalty programme
5. **Brand Leveraging**: YoggieBerrie could be expanded digitally
